Our Story

Observing a global oversight

we recognized Iraq's vibrant culture lacked representation. Foreign narratives drove our own heritage. Determined to change this, five passionate individuals came together, aiming to showcase Mesopotamia's true essence directly from the source.

Embracing our responsibility, we reclaim our narrative, sharing the warmth of Iraqi hospitality and the richness of our heritage. With hearts connected and barriers broken, we envision a more harmonious world.
